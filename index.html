Who We Are

Let it be known, Chives procure sounds that are eclectic even for today's standards of Psychedelic Rock. Combining zany vocals, abrasive percussion and burning guitar/bass partnerships. They'll take an immobile crowd and get them to bounce off the walls. Make no mistake, Chives are technical whilst remaining creatively sporadic.
